## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a copper Feng Shui gourd for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese gourd (hulu) shape - two connected round sections
- Made of shiny copper/bronze metal with golden-brown metallic sheen
- Small red rope/string attached at the top for hanging
- Subtle metallic reflections and highlights
- Small engraved patterns or Chinese characters on the surface (optional)
- Warm golden glow effect around it (representing protective Qi)

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear hanging or on a surface
- Approximately 20-30 pixels tall
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```

## 生成后处理

1. **背景透明化**: 确保背景完全透明
2. **尺寸校准**: 调整到适合游戏内显示的尺寸

## 游戏内使用

- **放置位置**: 悬挂在电线附近或放在电磁设备旁
- **净化效果**: 化解电磁辐射煞气
