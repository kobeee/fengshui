## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Shan Hai Zhen (山海镇) for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese Feng Shui protective talisman
- Rectangular wooden or metal plaque/tablet
- Features intricate carvings or paintings of mountains and seas
- Chinese characters "山海镇" prominently displayed
- Often has a red border or frame
- Used to deflect powerful external sha qi

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear hanging on wall
- Approximately 30-40 pixels wide, 20-30 pixels tall
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```
