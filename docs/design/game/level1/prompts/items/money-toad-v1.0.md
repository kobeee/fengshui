# 道具提示词 - 金蟾 (Money Toad v1.0)

| 属性 | 值 |

---

## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Jin Chan (Money Toad/金蟾) for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese three-legged toad statue
- Golden/yellow metallic body with coin in mouth
- Sitting on a pile of gold coins or ingots
- Red eyes and friendly appearance
- Often has 7 coin spines on its back (representing Big Dipper)
- Symbolizes wealth and prosperity in Feng Shui

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear sitting on a surface (table/shelf/floor)
- Approximately 20-30 pixels tall and wide
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```

---

## 生成后处理

1. **背景透明化**: 确保背景完全透明
2. **尺寸校准**: 调整到适合游戏内显示的尺寸

---

## 游戏内使用

- **放置位置**: 放在财位或门口附近
- **净化效果**: 增强财运，化解财位煞气
