## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Shi Gan Dang (石敢当/Stone Tablet) for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese protective stone monument
- Small rectangular stone pillar/tablet
- Chinese characters "石敢当" carved on the front
- Made of gray stone material
- May have simple decorative patterns
- Used to block and deflect negative energy from roads

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear on the floor or ground
- Approximately 15-25 pixels wide, 25-35 pixels tall
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```
