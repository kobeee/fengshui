## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Himalayan salt lamp for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Natural rock salt crystal shape - irregular, organic rock formation
- Warm orange-amber glow emanating from within
- Small wooden base at the bottom
- Soft light radiating outward (representing purification energy)
- The lamp should look like a rough-hewn pink/orange crystal

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear sitting on a surface (table/shelf/floor)
- Approximately 25-35 pixels tall
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Optional variations:
- Frame 1: Normal warm glow
- Frame 2: Brighter pulse (for animation potential)

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
- Include the warm glow effect as part of the sprite
```

---

## 生成后处理

1. **背景透明化**: 确保背景完全透明
2. **尺寸校准**: 调整到适合游戏内显示的尺寸
3. **发光效果**: 发光应该包含在精灵内

---

## 游戏内使用

- **放置位置**: 放在阴暗角落的桌面上
- **放置动画**: 轻轻放置并亮起
- **净化效果**: 黑暗角落被暖光驱散
