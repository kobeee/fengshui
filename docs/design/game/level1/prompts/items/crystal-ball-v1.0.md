## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a crystal ball for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Clear or white crystal sphere
- Sitting on a small wooden or metal stand
- Subtle internal light refractions and reflections
- Soft white/clear glow effect
- Looks like a pristine glass ball
- May have a slight rainbow shimmer effect

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear sitting on a surface (table/shelf/floor)
- Approximately 20-30 pixels in diameter including stand
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```
