## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Bagua mirror (八卦镜) for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese Feng Shui tool
- Circular mirror in the center
- Surrounded by eight trigrams (八卦) arranged in a circle
- Usually mounted on a wooden or metal frame
- Red or gold rim around the mirror
- The mirror reflects negative energy

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing

Size reference:
- When placed in room, should appear hanging on wall or placed on surface
- Approximately 25-35 pixels in diameter
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
```
