## 完整提示词

```text
Generate a pixel art sprite of a Feng Shui Dragon Turtle (龙龟) statue for an isometric puzzle game.

Object description:
- Traditional Chinese Feng Shui Dragon Turtle figurine
- Turtle body with dragon head - majestic and powerful appearance
- Carved from dark stone or bronze material (deep brown/black color)
- Shell has traditional Chinese pattern engravings
- Dragon head faces forward, mouth slightly open
- Small base/stand for desktop placement

Technical requirements:
- Isometric 45 degree angle, matching room perspective
- Transparent background (alpha channel)
- Hi-bit pixel art style, consistent with room background
- Max 32 colors in palette
- Clean pixel edges, no anti-aliasing
- Show figurine from 3/4 angle

Size reference:
- Desktop ornament size
- Approximately 25-35 pixels tall
- Scale appropriately for the room's perspective
- Should fit on a desk surface

Style notes:
- Traditional Chinese guardian beast aesthetic
- Auspicious and protective appearance
- Ancient artifact feel
- Subtle sheen on the material surface

Output:
- Single PNG with transparency
- Show figurine standing on implied surface
```

## 生成后处理

1. **背景透明化**: 确保背景完全透明
2. **尺寸校准**: 调整到适合游戏内显示的尺寸
3. **发光效果**: 可添加 subtle 的气场发光层用于净化效果

## 游戏内使用

- **放置位置**: 放置在座位后方或桌面作为靠山
- **放置动画**: 轻轻落在桌面上，出现气场波纹
- **净化效果**: 背后区域出现靠山气场效果
